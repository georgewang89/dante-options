MISSION

Why this company exists.

Millions of people live with conditions that remain undiagnosed or mismanaged because their doctors lack the complete genetic picture. Standard panels test a handful of genes. Targeted tests look only where they already suspect an answer.

Dante Labs exists to close that gap. We sequence the entire human genome at 30X clinical-grade coverage and deliver physician-ready reports that can change a diagnosis, redirect a treatment plan, or provide answers that years of specialist visits could not.

We believe every person deserves access to their complete genetic information, interpreted at the highest clinical standard, at a price that does not require institutional backing.
