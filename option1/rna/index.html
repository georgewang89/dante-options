WHY RNA

hub

DNA

Static. Complete instruction set.

Your DNA is the same in every cell and does not change over your lifetime. It is your complete genetic instruction set: the full code. Whole genome sequencing reads this code once and gives you your baseline.

RNA

Dynamic. Active output. Changes with disease, inflammation, medication.

Your RNA changes constantly. It reflects which genes are actually being expressed right now: what your body is doing, not just what it could do. RNA profiling captures this active state. It changes with disease, inflammation, medication, stress, and time.
